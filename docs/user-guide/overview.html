Overview

The User Guide is for those who have already gone through the Quick Start and have a first draft of their professional portfolio online. This section will help you learn how to more easily manage your portfolio.

You'll learn:

  • How to develop your professional portfolio in the cloud (preferred) or locally.
  • The features of Markdown, Jekyll, and Bootstrap that can make your portfolio shine.
